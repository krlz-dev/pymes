Plant Care

Care Tips for Your Plants

Watering Guide

Most indoor plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Check soil moisture before watering - stick your finger 1 inch deep.

Light Requirements

Match your plants to available light. Most houseplants thrive in bright, indirect light. Rotate plants regularly for even growth.

Feeding Tips

Feed your plants during growing season (spring-summer) with balanced fertilizer. Reduce feeding in fall and winter when growth slows.
