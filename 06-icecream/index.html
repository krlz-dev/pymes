Our Story

Scooping Happiness Since 1998

What started as a small ice cream cart in the park has grown into a beloved local chain with 15 stores across the city. Our founder, Maria Rodriguez, believed that ice cream should bring joy - and that means using only the best ingredients.

We source our dairy from local farms, use real fruits, and create unique flavors that you won't find anywhere else. Every scoop is made with love and a commitment to quality.
